Causes of different types of pollution

  • Land Pollution

    • Deforestation
    • Soil erosion
    • Agricultural activites
    • Mining activities
    • Overuse of landfills
    • Industrialization
    • Littering
    • Improper disposal of toxic materials
    • Terrestrial oil spills

  • Water Pollution

    • Industrial Waste being dumped into water bodies/water ways
    • Domestic effluent entering water
    • Improper handling of sewage
    • Fertilizers and Pesticides rrunning off into oceans
    • Oil spills
    • Solid waste being disposed of in rivers

  • Air Pollution

    • Forest fires
    • Burning of garbage
    • Combustion in engines
    • Dust emission from cement and chemical plant
    • Combustion of fossil fuels
    • Use of aerosols
    • Chlorofluoro Carbons (CFCs) and Halons

  • Light Pollution

    • Excessive use of lights
    • House hold lights
    • Urbanization
    • Cars and other vehicles
    • Night-time lighting
    • Poor infrastructure planning

  • Noise Pollution

    • Transport/Traffic Noise
    • Neighborhood Noise
    • Industrial Noise
    • Industrialization
    • Construction sites
    • Social events and gatherings

  • Thermal Pollution

    • Water as a Cooling Agent in Power, Manufacturing and Industrial Plants
    • Water runoff from paved surfaces
    • Soil erosion
    • Deforestation
    • Lightening, Volcanoes, Geothermal vents and Hot Springs

  • Radioacive Pollution

    • Nuclear weapons testing
    • Radioactive waste from nuclear power plants
    • Nuclear power plant accidents and explosions
    • Nuclear warfare
    • Extraction of naturally occurring radioactive materials
    • Cosmic radiation
