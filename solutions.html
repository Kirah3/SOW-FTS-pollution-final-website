Solutions and Preventaton Methods

for Each Type of Pollution

  • Land Pollution

    • Establishment of suitable sites for the disposal of toxic waste
    • Reuse of materials that can be used more than once
    • Reduce the amount of non-biodegradable waste produced
    • Recycling of materials

  • Water Pollution

    • Better systems to prevent and deal with oil spills
    • Proper construction and regular maintenance of sewage treatmentplants
    • Reservoirs where industrial effluent can be treated and broken down to remove harmful chemcals before the water is released into the water ways
    • Reduce plastic consumption
    • Biological pest control in place of pesticides
    • Education of the public on the consequences of water pollution

  • Air Pollution

    • Carpooling
    • Improving and Using Pubic Transport
    • Eco-friendly fuels like CNG in place of fossil fuels
    • Reforestation
    • Strict laws to govern the way in which factories dispose of pollutants
    • Alternative forms of energy instead of combustion of fossil fuels
    • Tall chimneys to expell polluting gases into the upper atmosphere to be dispersed rather into populated areas
    • Public education on why this is a problem
    • Reduction of uncontrolled fires

  • Light Pollution

    • Use of warm lighting in place of cold lighting
    • Light shields
    • Turn offlights during certain periods
    • Use of eco-friendly lights
    • Motion sensor activated lights
    • Education on the harmful effect of light pollution

  • Noise Pollution

    • Avoid noisy leisure activities
    • Insulate homes with oise cancelling materials
    • Educate individuals on the harm caused by noise pollution
    • Create protected areas wherein excessive noise is prohibited
    • Laws governing the noise levels allowed in specific areas

  • Thermal Pollution

    • Cooling ponds
    • Cooling towers
    • Artificial Lakes
    • Water recycling

  • Radioacive Pollution

    • Allow for a decaying period to pass to reduce the toxicity of waste before it is disposed of
    • Decontaminate anything that came into contact with radioactive materials
    • Laws govrning the disposal of nuclear waste
    • Use of safe, renewable energy in place of nuclear power
    • Regularly check and moniter radiation levels of both urban and industrial environments
