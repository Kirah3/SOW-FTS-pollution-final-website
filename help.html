Things you can do at home to lowwer your carbon footprint and reduce the pollution you cause

  • Turn off lights when not in use
  • Reuse anything that can be used more than once
  • Recycle
  • Use fans instead of air conditioning units
  • Walk or bike when possible instead of using cars
  • Buy only what you need to prevent wastage
  • Avoid littering
  • Dispose of batteries and electrical products properly
  • Plan trees
  • Use the minimum amount of bleach/detergent when washing clothes
  • Avoid excessive use of artificial pesticies, herbicides and fertilizers
  • Maintaintain you car well to prevent leekage of oil, antifreeze or coolant
